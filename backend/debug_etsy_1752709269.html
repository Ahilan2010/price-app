HOW IT’S MADE:
……………………….
Each ring is handmade from start to finish. Raw 24k solid gold is melted by hand to bring it to 14k solid gold and pulled to its final thickness of 18 gauge wire. Every purchase is strictly handmade to order, and no two are exactly alike. The wire is shaped, soldered, sized, and polished all by hand. This is a professional handmade ring and not manufactured in a factory.

ABOUT US:
……………………….
Aubare was born out of a desire to create contemporary, affordable high quality jewelry that add value to your life. I craft all my pieces by hand from start to finish using solid gold (10K, 14K 18K). The result...dainty, minimalist jewelry you'll never want to, or have to, take off.
